DODOLEIRO'S
JOURNEY

EARLY BEGINNINGS AND RISE IN ITALY. DODOLEIRO’S JOURNEY STARTED AT JUST 13, SPINNING TRACKS AT A CLASS PARTY, QUICKLY EVOLVING INTO A CAREER SPANNING OVER 1,000 THRILLING CLUB NIGHTS ACROSS EUROPE. ESTABLISHING HIMSELF FIRMLY WITHIN TUSCANY’S VIBRANT NIGHTLIFE, HE PERFORMED AT RENOWNED VENUES INCLUDING LA CAPANNINA DI FRANCESCHI, OPENING FOR LEGENDARY DJS LIKE GIGI D'AGOSTINO, AND IGNITED DANCEFLOORS AT FARUK CLUB, BEACH AND J'ADORE CLUB.
‍
INTERNATIONAL EXPANSION. BETWEEN 2017 AND 2023, AS A RESIDENT DJ AT NAIF DISCOTHEQUE, HE SHARED THE STAGE WITH PROMINENT RAP FIGURES SUCH AS LOWLOW, BORO BORO, RANDOM, AND LUDWIG.
NOW BASED IN PARIS, HE CONTINUES TO GROW HIS INTERNATIONAL PRESENCE, HEADLINING POPULAR EVENTS IN PARIS, LUXEMBOURG, AND IBIZA, CAPTIVATING DIVERSE AUDIENCES ACROSS EUROPE.

SOUND DESIGN
& MORE

MASTERFUL MIXING TECHNIQUES. WITH OVER 13 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE BEHIND THE DECKS, DODOLEIRO HAS DEVELOPED SOPHISTICATED MIXING SKILLS THAT ALLOW HIM TO SEAMLESSLY BLEND HOUSE AND AFRO-HOUSE RHYTHMS. HIS EXPERT USE OF TRANSITIONS AND CAREFULLY CURATED TRACK SELECTION CREATE AN ENERGIZING ATMOSPHERE, APPEAL TO IGNITE ANY DANCEFLOOR.

MUSICAL ROOTS AND VERSATILITY. DODOLEIRO’S VERSATILITY IS ROOTED IN HIS EARLY DAYS SPINNING HIP-HOP AND LATIN GENRES. OVER TIME, THIS FOUNDATION HAS EVOLVED INTO THE VIBRANT, DYNAMIC STYLE THAT CHARACTERIZES HIS CURRENT PERFORMANCES. HIS SETS ARE IMMERSIVE MUSICAL JOURNEYS MARKED BY DISTINCTIVE RHYTHMS, SEAMLESS COMBINATIONS, AND CONTAGIOUS ENERGY.
